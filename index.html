Contourer
Visualise 2-input, 2-output functions the Contour way

A GPU-accelerated visualisation tool for mesh transformations, electric fields and complex analysis. Animate your own contour plots with just a little bit of code. Browser-based and mobile-friendly, so no downloads required. Contourer is open-sourced on Github.

Finished, Mid 2016

LightPC
A 29-bit Computer for The Powder Toy

Lazy man's megaprocessor that you can actually play with, right now. Built with PHOT technology, which has been outdated by ARAY/DRAY technology, but nobody has made one with ARAY/DRAY yet because nobody seems to play The Powder Toy anymore. The compiler, a creation timelapse and lots more goodies are available for your enjoyment.

Finished, Mid 2014

Fourier Transform Visualiser
Watch a function being built from sinusoids

Built back in the day when Flash was still a thing, this tool illustrates how a function can be decomposed into sinusoids through the Fourier Discrete Cosine Transform. Use the mouse to draw a function, then press spacebar repeatedly to reconstruct it from sinusoids. Hold the spacebar down to accelerate. Press "Z" to view the spectrum and "R" to reset.

Finished, Mid 2013
